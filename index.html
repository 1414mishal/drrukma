General ENT Questions

Is earwax removal safe at the clinic?

Yes, we use professional micro-suction or irrigation methods that are much safer and more effective than using home cotton buds.

What causes frequent sinus headaches?

Inflammation of the sinus cavities due to allergies or infections can cause pressure. A consultation helps identify the specific trigger.

When should I see an ENT for a sore throat?

If a sore throat lasts more than a week, is accompanied by a high fever, or causes difficulty swallowing, you should see an expert.

Do you treat vertigo?

Yes, many balance issues are related to the inner ear. We perform assessments to diagnose the type of vertigo and provide treatment.
